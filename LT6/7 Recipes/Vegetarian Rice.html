Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 (14 ounce) can petite-diced tomatoes
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 (15 ounce) can no-salt added black beans, rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons Salsa and/or shredded Mexican blend cheese

Directions

  • Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
    Add rice and cook, stirring, until starting to brown, about 3 minutes.
  • Add onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper; cook, stirring, until fragrant; 1 to 2 minutes
  • Add onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper; cook, stirring, until fragrant; 1 to 2 minutes
  • Cover and cook until the liquid has been absorbed, 18 to 20 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and fluff with a fork. Gently stir in beans and corn. Cover and let stand for 10 minutes.
  • Stir in cilantro and serve with salsa and cheese, if desired.
